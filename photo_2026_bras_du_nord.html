Sentier des Falaises, Valllée Bras-du-Nord

Massive boulders and flora along a steep ascent.

The trek up the Sentier des Falaises is well trimmed but has many stones to maneuver around and above. This made it difficult for me to pay much mind to the scenery on the way up as I was paying a lot more attention to my feet and stamina, but I really saw the spectacular nature of it on the way down with the boulders and their foliage catching the late rays of the sun. The better shots seem to have primarially been family photos and the macro images I've included in this gallery; hope you enjoy!
