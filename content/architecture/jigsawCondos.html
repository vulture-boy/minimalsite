Jigsaw Condos

2015

A condominium proposal for the Vanier neighbourhood of Ottawa. The assembly objective was to provide both north and south facing light to as many units within the 'elbow' as possible. A 'diamond' shutter system was programmed and designed to adapt to lighting conditions throughout the day. The condominium was originally designed to interlock with another student's project (in white silhouette). Included in a studio exhibit and small-run paperback titled "You're So Vanier".
