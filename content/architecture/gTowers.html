Gossiping Towers

2016 | In collaboration with Shawn Duke.

The Gossiping Towers urban renewal project aims to revitalize one of Ottawa's lower income neighbourhoods into a high density tech hub and act as the catalyst for the development of a tram car transporation system across the city.

The tallest of the projects, the Gateway Towers, boldly proclaims the hub's Constructivist ideals; the twins are designed to demonstrate to developers the potential to use the tramline's existing and future structures as frameworks for condominium development. The project promises to be profitable for both the city and prospective developers alike, while encouraging urban growth along the tramline route.

The project was featured on the cover of ARCHITECT magazine's September 2016 issue.
