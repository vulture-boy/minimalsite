OMX Interface Redesign

2019 | In collaboration with Feng Yuan, April De Zen, Zuriel T. C. de la Cruz, and Ajaz Hussain

As part of a research endeavor at OCAD University's Visual Analytics Lab, I prepared and developed a revised layout for OMX's procurement platform.

Emphasis was placed on improving the usability of the Contract Lifecycle Management platform's tabular sections with a new visualization panel, filtering system and emphasized key figures to improve the overall user experience and the efficiency of the platform's components.

See also: Feng Yuan's contributions and breakdown of early project timeline.
