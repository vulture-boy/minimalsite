Tyson Moll - Toronto Snowstorm 2025

Toronto Snowstorm, 2025

Documenting downtown on the snowiest day of the winter.

When I think of snow in Toronto, I usually think of slush. Compared to other major northern Canadian cities where major unmelting snowfall is the norm, great blizzards like these often catch the city offguard. Richard and I hiked out along Dundas Street from Yonge Square to Spadina Avenue. We witnessed all manner of commuters doing their best to make it through the city as well as some unfortunate errors like the van that parked a little too close to the tracks and blocked five streetcars. These are the photos from our hike.
