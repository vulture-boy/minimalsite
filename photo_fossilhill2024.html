FOSSIL HILL NATURE RESERVE, 2024

Images of the rock shelves and fauna along the Fossil Hill Trail on Manitoulin Island, Ontario

The Fossil Hill trail of Fossil Hill Nature Reserve runs within the escarpment of Manitoulin island. It features low-rise cliffs and thin soil and bears various beautiful moss and fungi varieties. This gallery also features a few Sandhill Cranes spotted migrating in the area.
