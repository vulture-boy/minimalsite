Tyson Moll - Blurry Vancouver Aquarium 2015

Blurry Vancouver Aquarium, 2015

Photos captured in low light at the Vancouver Aquarium.

While reviewing photos from a cross Canada roadtrip in 2015, I came across a set of photos from the aquarium that have a bit of an amateur's touch. But in spite of that, I think there's something beautiful about them... I have to remind myself that even when a photo goes out of focus, balance or detail, it can still be appreciated for qualities such as motion, the blending of colors, and simply the memories of the moment. I'd like to take some of these ideas and bring them into future photographs, but with intention.
Aside from that, I love the subject matter; the Vancouver Aquarium is a lovely space. Many marvelous creatures from all trees of life on spectacle that I would never see without a ticket beyond the comforts of the city. Colors, weightlessness, different states of being, different lives.
